Boomerang Hunter

Instructions

Meet Sydney:

Play as Sydney, an adventurous Kangaroo, as he explores the Australian Outback in search of boomerangs. But be careful, this area is home to dozens of abandoned mines and the leftover dynamite can be very dangerous.

How To Play:

Use the left and right arrow keys to move left and right.

Use the up arrow to jump.

Collect as many boomerangs as you can.

Avoid the bouncing dynamite!
